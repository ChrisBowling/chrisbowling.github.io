ABOUT ME

Hi! My name's Chris Bowling. I'm a reporter, designer and photographer from Cincinnati, Ohio currently studying journalism at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.

WORDS

I love hearing and telling stories. I've worked on newspapers everywhere from the university level to small towns and major cities. I've written about a Yazidi man's struggle to make it to America, Nebraska's battle over the death penalty, how a losing Husker team affects gameday business and the deep wounds of a small-town park.
